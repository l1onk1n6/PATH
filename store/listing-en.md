## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
PATH is the simplest way to build professional job application documents. Create your resume, cover letter, and complete application kit in minutes and export everything as a print-ready PDF.

**What PATH can do**

• **15+ professional templates** — from classic to modern. Switch with one click, pick your own accent color.

• **Resume editor** — Personal info, work experience, education, skills, languages, projects, certificates. Reorder any entry freely.

• **Cover letter** — Built-in editor with recipient, subject, body, and closing. Styled to match your resume.

• **One-click PDF export** — Print-ready, pixel-perfect, under 5 MB. Perfect for online applications and paper mail.

• **Complete application kit** — Cover letter, resume, and uploaded references/certificates merged into a single PDF file.

• **Multiple people & kits** — Create separate application kits for different positions. Personal info stays consistent everywhere.

• **Cloud sync** — Your data is stored securely; continue on any device.

**Why PATH?**

PATH is for people who want to send a strong application without spending hours fighting formatting. No sketchy template sites, no watermarks, no fine print. Just type in your content, pick a template, export.

**Privacy**

Your data is stored only in your personal account. No third-party access. Passwords are encrypted. You can export your data or delete your account at any time (GDPR Art. 20).

Built by pixmatic in Switzerland.
